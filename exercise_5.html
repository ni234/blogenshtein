In the example above, the background property is identified in the transition-property value. Here the background property is the only property that will change over the duration of 1 second in a linear fashion. Any other properties included when changing an elementâ€™s state, but not included within the transition-property value, will not receive the transition behaviors as set by the transition-duration or transition-timing-function properties. If multiple properties need to be transitioned they may be comma separated within the transition-property value. Additionally, the keyword value all may be used to transition all properties of an element.
